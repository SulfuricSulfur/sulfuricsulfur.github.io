Notes

Notes about the portfolio and citation to any external sources.

Above and Beyond

I used Javascript via bootstrap. I used the drop down bar and hamburger bar for the navigation when the size of the screen is small enough or on mobile. I also implemented a carousel system. There are also multiple carousels on one page. Most of the images on the site were taken by me or from a project. The background image on the home page was taken by me when I visited Indonesia. Glyphicons/icons were added along with the text on the contact me page to give some visual image to relate to the text and what it corresponds to.

Challenges and Improvements

Getting the navbar to be a different color than the Bootstrap navbar was a challenge. There are a lot of aspects that go into the navbar, and changing the color of each little piece was tedious and a challenge to constantly be changing them. It was also a challenge to get the right color scheme I wanted. I had to search all over for colors that I wanted, and even then I still changed the hues. There was also the challenge of aligning the columns and the divs. Sometimes when I change one it would also change another that was next to it. I had to fiddle quite a bit with them in order to have them sit in the position I wanted and scale with the screen. I would also work on the home page and make it look more interesting.

CSS Framework

I used the Bootstrap columns and rows to organize the layout of the pages. There are also carousels in order to display all the images for the game without taking up a lot of space on the page by showing them individually. Made the featurette-divider bar have more padding between the top elements in order to spread elements out a little bit more. Added specifications to any image inside col-lg-6 would be displayed in the center of the grid element. When making the carousels, I had to make it so that the images were centered. I made it so that the images of the inner carousel would be in the center. I also made it so that the margin between the columns to have gaps between each other. I also changed the color scheme of the website rather than the basic colors given by Bootstrap. This goes for the navbar and other aspects.
