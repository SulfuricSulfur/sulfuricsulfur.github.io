Exor-Sister

This is a project that I helped work on for the RIT 2016 Global Game Jam. The theme was "Ritual!" . We did a parody of cooking mama where you had to play minigames in order to exorcise a demon. I was involved with art and the locust minigame. Team size was 6. Members were: Rochelle Cohn, Sam Levey, James Spinelli, Alex Lobl, JD Kelly, and Isaac Kelman.

Good Cop, Bat Cop

A gritty cop, who happens to be a bat, is tracking down one of the most illustrious criminals. It's up to him to use his echolocation to traverse the warehouse and catch Robber Rat.


Good Cop, Bat Cop is a stealth based game made during the 2017 Global Game Jam. The theme was “Waves”. The game requires the player to seek out and capture Robber Rat without giving away the player’s location. The player must use their “sonar” in order to light up and see their surroundings.


Team size was seven. My task was helping to implement the AI, drawing the 2D art, and make and changes to the game for balancing reasons.
