Fish and Birds: Autonomous Agents Final

This project was made as a final project in my Interactive Media Development class. The goal of the final was to make an environment with multiple agents in it that interact with the environment and each other. For this, the group of birds flock together while also pursuing the fish. The fish follow a leader fish who is seeking out different waypoints.

*CONTROLS FOR PROJECT:

C - change camera
W - Move control fish forwards
S - Move control fish backwards
A - Turn control fish left
D - Turn control fish right
END - Secret

Paul and the Yum-Yum Crew: The Eternal Search

This is a game I worked on with a group of 5 including myself. All art except the main menu were made by the team's artist. The game was made in Monogame. The game is a 2D puzzle-platforming game where the player can switch between a ghost state to phase through objects and a physical state for platforming. I worked primarily on physics, enemies, debugging, and managing team deadlines.

Countdown to Midnight

Countdown to Midnight is a cold war themed card game in which the players try to prevent the shadow government from causing a nuclear war. Players try to prevent the apocalypse by tactically removing unrest in their country and around the world. They are also trying to expose the shadow government, gaining information from hotspots of unrest at the cost of increasing the tensions in those areas. Players also use politics and policies to reduce unrest as well as help out their teammates. The team size was 5.
