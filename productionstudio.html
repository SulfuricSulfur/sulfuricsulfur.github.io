Kill The Streamer

Ever want to know what it was like to actively battle Twitch Chat? Find out! Kill the Streamer will read the chat of a Twitch chat room and spawn enemies based on the commands that the users say, in real time. The game currently features:

  • Over 15 different weapons, each highly unique.
  • 6 different enemies, each based on a global Twitch emote.

The game is currently under development by a group of students from the Rochester Institute of Technology. Some small polishes to still come, but it's otherwise complete.

Download can be found at: KillTheStreamer.itch.io

Scale

This is a group project that was made for a Production Studio. The concept of the game was that you are an adventurer whose soul was split into two parts. The other half of the soul became corrupted and morphed into a dragon that lives atop of a castle. You goal is to scale the tower by gaining abilities in order to reunite with your other half. The game was presented as part of Imagine RIT. The group total size was eight with four artists and four programmers. I was mainly in charge of working on the vents to make the player float, gliding, script activation, moving platforms and physics. I also worked on camera movement, and lights.
