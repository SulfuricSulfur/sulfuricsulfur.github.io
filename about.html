About Rochelle Cohn

This page is devoted to information and facts about me.

General Information

I was born in the United States and I moved to London, England when I was about two years old. I received a duel citizen-ship for the United States and for England. I lived there till I was about ten years old at which point I moved back to the United States, more specifically, New York. I applied and got in to Rochester Institute of Technology for Game Design and Development and an immersion in Creative Writing. Currently I am a 4th year undergraduate.


I am currently the events director for Tora-Con, an anime and gaming convention run by the students and alumni of RIT. I am constantly revising schedules and plans in order to help make Tora-Con better and better.
